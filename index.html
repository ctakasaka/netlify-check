Oh! Hello there :3

  1. Press the Windows key (this is called the "super" key)!
  2. Type in cmd and press enter, this will launch the command-line.
  3. Type ipconfig in the command-line window
  4. Look for the like that says ipv4... and get the number at the end (it should look like 192.168."some other numbers")
  5. Now start the minecraft world and press the open on LAN buttn.
  6. There should be a number in your minecraft chat, that number is the port that your minecraft server is being hosted on.
  7. The person joining your server neesd t go to the multiplayer screen in minecraft and press direct connect.
  8. They then need to put in that number from the command-line window, then a colon (:), then the port number (should look something like 192.168.0.1:55678)
  9. That's it! Now press connect and you should be playing together in your own personal minecraft world!
